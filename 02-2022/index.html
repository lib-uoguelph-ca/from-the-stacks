Welcome to our newsletter!

We are pleased to launch the U of G Library's inaugural monthly newsletter - From the Stacks. Here you will find information about library services, events, workshops, featured collections, as well as useful tips. This issue focuses on information that will support library users' return to face-to-face teaching and learning, as well as our featured collection in celebration of Black History Month.

Library Feature: Writing Services

Whether you're writing your first university paper, completing your doctoral dissertation, or writing a research article for publication, Writing Services is here for you!

Writing Services can work with you to develop your writing skills by:

  • Providing feedback on your drafts
  • Answering questions about writing in specific disciplines or genres
  • Recommending strategies for organizing your ideas and structuring your essay, thesis, paper, or chapter
  • Advising on clarity, cohesion, and style

And that's not all - Writing Services also offers support for English as an Additional Language and French writing appointments. Find out how Writing Services can help you develop your writing this semester.
