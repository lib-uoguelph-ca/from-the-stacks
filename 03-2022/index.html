FAQ: What if I can’t find a resource in Omni?

We often get questions from students and researchers asking what to do if they can’t find a resource they need in Omni.

If you can’t find what you’re looking for in Omni, try ordering it through RACER!

RACER is an interlibrary loan service available to U of G students, staff, and faculty. Fill out a RACER form with your request, and you’ll typically get your book in 10 business days.

Library Accessibility Services

Library Accessibility Services (LAS) at the library are here to provide adaptive software and technology supports to students who are registered with Student Accessibility Services.

Here are a few of the services LAS provides:

  • Alternative formats of textbooks and other materials
  • Instruction on using the library catalogue and other electronic reference tools
  • Access to adaptive software programs on the Access Labs’ computers
  • Loaning digital recorders, smart pens, and tablets
  • Access to accessible study rooms
Learn more about LAS
