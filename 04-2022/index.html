Good luck on your exams!

The end of the semester is now in sight as we officially enter exam season, and we’re here to help you reach the finish line and achieve your goals. Whether you'd like advice on a research paper, suggestions of effective study strategies, or tips on how to balance your studies and your wellness, we have resources for you.

A reminder that we require medical-style masks at all times in the library and our vaccination policy remains in effect until the end of term. We appreciate your continued cooperation. If you prefer studying with friends, good news – we have lifted our physical distancing requirements! Group study rooms can once again be booked according to their capacity limits.

Ask Us Desk

The Ask Us Desk is your first stop in discovering all the library’s services! Whether you’re looking for a resource, have questions about library services, or need help with your library account, the Ask Us Desk staff can help.

Ask us about:

  • Library services, workshops, and events
  • How to find information, recommend sources for research, and suggest research strategies
  • Your library account
  • Borrowing library materials, course reserves, and interlibrary loan and document delivery services
  • Group study rooms bookings and graduate student study offices
  • Printing, photocopying, and scanning

Find the Ask Us Desk on the first floor of the library. You can also chat with us online, email us, or call us at 519-824-4120 x53618.
